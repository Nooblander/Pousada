Welcome to Pousada Tendawa, hosted with care by Suellem Esquerdo. Tucked away in the heart of Alter do Chão, our bed and breakfast is designed for travelers seeking top-tier comfort, a central location, and authentic Amazon vibes — without the noise.

Alter do Chão is home to some of the most spectacular freshwater beaches on Earth. Staying somewhere quiet, central, and meticulously hosted completely shifts your journey.

What awaits you at Pousada Tendawa:

  • Central location — stroll effortlessly down to the waterfront
  • Quiet, pristine, air-conditioned suites
  • Complimentary fresh tropical breakfast included daily
  • High-speed Wi-Fi access & easy check-in experience
  • Experienced local hosting with deep roots in the region

Discover the Magic of the Amazon

Whether you want to sail along the crystal-clear waters of the Tapajós River, trek through lush national forests, or watch an unforgettable sunset at Ponta do Cururu, our team is here to help you organize the perfect itinerary. Experience the best boat tours, local cuisine, and hidden spots that only the locals know about.
